Our Company Policy

Milky Royal, a Turkish-British co-investment; on its way to ‘Food Production in Pharmaceutical Sensitivity’, It has chosen the city of Aydın, where is the dairy region of Turkey, as an investment region. It continues its milk powder, whey powder, protein powder and other dairy powder production activities in an open area of 17.000 square meters and a closed area of 10.500 square meters in Aydın Ortaklar Organized Industrial Zone. With the slogan of 'Powder Of Perfection', it takes only the best quality whey and produces high quality whey powder and protein powder that can be used in all food products, sports products, baby foods.
